Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Therapy Management System. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us.

How do I register a new patient?

To register a new patient, go to the Patient Management section and click on "Add Patient". Fill out the required information and submit the form.

How can I create a new therapy plan?

Navigate to the Therapy Plans page and click on "Create Plan". You will be prompted to enter details about the new plan and save it.

What should I do if I encounter an error in the system?

If you encounter an error, please try refreshing the page first. If the issue persists, report it using the support form or contact our support team directly.

How do I view the progress reports of patients?

To view progress reports, go to the Progress Reports section. You can select the patient and view their report history.

Can I customize therapy plans for individual patients?

Yes, you can customize therapy plans based on individual patient needs. Use the "Edit" option in the Therapy Plans section to modify existing plans.

Still have questions? Contact us for further assistance.
