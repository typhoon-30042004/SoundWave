Introduction

The Therapy Management System is designed to streamline the management of patient therapy sessions, documentation, and reporting. This guide will walk you through the essential features and provide tips on how to make the most of the system.

Getting Started

To get started, log in to the system using your assigned credentials. Once logged in, you will be directed to the main dashboard, where you can access various features such as patient management, therapy plans, session documentation, and more.

Using the Dashboard

The dashboard is your central hub for accessing all features of the system. Here, you can quickly view upcoming sessions, recent activities, and system notifications.

  • Quick Links: Access frequently used features directly from the dashboard.
  • Recent Activities: Keep track of the latest updates and changes made in the system.
  • System Notifications: Stay informed about system updates, scheduled maintenance, and other important alerts.

Patient Management

The patient management module allows you to add, edit, and view patient records. You can also assign therapy plans and monitor patient progress from this section.

  • Add New Patient: Enter patient details, assign a therapist, and start a new therapy plan.
  • Edit Patient Details: Update patient information as needed.
  • View Patient History: Access a complete history of the patientâ€™s therapy sessions and progress reports.

Creating Therapy Plans

Design personalized therapy plans for each patient based on their specific needs. You can choose from a variety of templates or create a custom plan from scratch.

  • Select Template: Choose from pre-designed templates or customize your own plan.
  • Assign to Patient: Assign the therapy plan to a specific patient and schedule sessions.
  • Monitor Progress: Track the patient's progress and make adjustments to the plan as needed.

Session Documentation

Document each therapy session in real-time, ensuring that all notes, observations, and outcomes are accurately recorded.

  • Real-Time Documentation: Enter session details during or immediately after the session.
  • View Session History: Review previous session notes and adjust future sessions accordingly.
  • Upload Attachments: Include relevant files or documents as part of the session record.

Viewing Progress Reports

Generate and view detailed progress reports for each patient, helping you to track improvements and identify areas that may require additional focus.

  • Generate Reports: Create comprehensive reports based on session documentation and patient progress.
  • Share Reports: Share reports with patients or other healthcare providers as needed.
  • Customize Reports: Tailor the report format to meet specific requirements.

Supervision and Reviews

Supervisors can review session documentation, monitor therapy plans, and provide feedback to therapists.

  • Review Documentation: Ensure all session documentation meets required standards.
  • Monitor Plans: Keep track of all therapy plans assigned to patients under your supervision.
  • Provide Feedback: Offer constructive feedback to therapists to improve patient outcomes.

System Settings

Customize your experience with the Therapy Management System by adjusting settings such as notifications, privacy preferences, and more.

  • Profile Settings: Update your personal information and change your password.
  • Notification Preferences: Choose how and when you receive system notifications.
  • Security Settings: Adjust privacy and security settings to protect patient data.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using the system, refer to the following troubleshooting tips:

  • Login Issues: Ensure your username and password are correct. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.
  • Slow Performance: Clear your browser's cache or try using a different browser.
  • Feature Not Working: Check for system updates or contact support if the issue persists.
  • Other Issues: Contact our support team for assistance.
